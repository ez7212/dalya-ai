# Dalya AI — Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
**Primary — UAE Property Buyers**
Qualified buyers (AED 30k+ HH income) researching off-plan resale units digitally before committing. Mix of Emirati nationals, Gulf expats, Western expats, South Asian expats. Prefer WhatsApp over calls. Decision horizon 2–12 months. Trust trigger: specific numbers, clear terms, zero sales pressure.

**Secondary — Off-Plan Resale Sellers**
Investors looking to exit before handover. Already hold an SPA, know their payment %, have a target price. Want reach (Property Finder, Bayut) without brokerage friction. Trust trigger: professional listing, verifiable data, fast lead response.

**Context of use**: Mobile-first, often reviewing during work hours. Data-dense content (payment schedules, AED figures, NOC status) must be scannable at a glance.

---

### Brand Personality
**Three words**: Precise. Inviting. Modern.

**Emotional goal — first 3 seconds**: Instant utility. Buyers should see listings and start asking questions immediately. No preamble, no teaser, no pressure.

**Voice**: Direct and knowledgeable without condescension. Warm but not overfamiliar. Gets to the number fast. Never vague superlatives.

**Tagline**: "Property intelligence. No pressure."

---

### Aesthetic Direction

**Approved direction**: Navy/gold dark theme — confirmed. The `#0F1923` deep dark with `#C9A96E` gold accents and `#4A7C6F` sage trust signals is the visual language.

**Closest references**: Linear.app and Dropbox — clean, precise, every element earns its place. Modern PropTech energy, not SaaS marketing site, not luxury real estate brochure.

**Anti-references (explicitly avoid)**:
- Property Finder / Bayut — crowded portals, orange/red CTAs, broker-first UX
- Generic SaaS landing page — gradient hero blobs, 3-column feature grids, laptop stock photos
- Crypto / Web3 — neon accents, glowing borders, aggressive motion
- Corporate real estate brochure — marble textures, gold-as-decoration, "luxury" plastered everywhere

**Theme**: Dark mode primary. A light mode may come later but is not a current priority.

**RTL / Arabic**: Phase 3+ — English-first for now. Use logical CSS properties (`padding-inline`, `margin-block`) so RTL won't require a rewrite.

---

### Design Principles

1. **Data is the hero**
   The listing card, the price, the payment percentage — these are the product. They should be immediately visible above the fold. Never hide the actual platform behind marketing copy.

2. **Gold is a signal, not wallpaper**
   Use `#C9A96E` purposefully: CTAs, AED figures, verified badges, active states. Every time a user sees gold it should mean something. If it appears as decoration, its trust value collapses.

3. **Marketplace, not marketing**
   The UI should feel like you've arrived at a real, working product — not a teaser for one. Prioritise functional surfaces (listing grids, dashboards, advisor chat) over cinematic hero sections.

4. **Precision through restraint**
   No borders for structure — use tonal surface layers (`#1A2B3C`, `#2E4057`, `#0F1923`). No pure black shadows — use Ink-tinted ambients. Generous spacing is respect for dense data, not decoration.

5. **Modern PropTech, not traditional brokerage**
   The platform should feel closer to Linear or Stripe than to a Dubai real estate agency website. Clean typography hierarchy, tight editorial tracking on headlines, JetBrains Mono for all AED figures and percentages.
