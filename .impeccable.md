# Dalya AI — Design Context

> Canonical source: the `## Design Context` and brand sections in `CLAUDE.md`, plus `brand/` (start at `brand/BRAND.md`). This file mirrors the current B2B direction. The previous navy/gold consumer-marketplace design is **retired** — do not use it.

## Design Context

### Users
**Individual agents (primary daily users)** — entry-level agents and team leads at Dubai brokerages. Mobile-first, WhatsApp-adjacent, hours of daily use. Trust trigger: the tool closes more deals and makes the day easier; never replaces them.

**Brokerage owners (secondary — the buyer)** — owners of small-to-medium Dubai brokerages who care about team performance and revenue per agent. Primarily desktop, dense analytical work. Trust trigger: real telemetry, RERA + PDPL compliance, demonstrable revenue lift per agent.

**Buyers (consumed via the bot, not customers)** — UAE-based qualified buyers messaging via WhatsApp/portal. Multilingual (EN/AR/RU/HI). Their experience reflects on the agent and brokerage.

### Brand Personality
**Three words (priority order): Trustworthy. Calm. Sharp.**

Voice: specific numbers, named sources, no exaggeration; quiet by omission; useful at the moment of decision. Present-tense and concrete (Attio-like) when describing what the system does.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Light-default with a parallel dark mode.** Slate-blue accent, neutral-scale workhorse, semantic color only when status is meaningful. References: **Attio** (product surface), **Notion** (white-space discipline), **Linear** (color discipline). Quiet-by-omission — no decorative ornament, no Dubai exoticization, no luxury-PropTech residue.

**Anti-references:** Property Finder / Bayut crowded UI, generic SaaS pastel, tech-bro brutalism, the previous consumer dark-luxury Dalya (navy `#0F1923` + gold `#C9A96E` — fully retired).

### Design Principles
1. **Empowerment, not replacement** — visibly augment the agent; the relationship, judgment, negotiation, and close stay with the agent.
2. **Trust through specifics** — real numbers, named sources, compliance attribution per listing's brokerage (Dalya is software, not the RERA licensee).
3. **Calm by omission** — generous space, no notification theatre, accent color only where it means something.
4. **Sharp at the moment of decision** — the brief reads in twenty seconds because that's how long an agent has before the call.
