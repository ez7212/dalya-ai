How we ship

Design partnerships, not enterprise sales.

We work with one brokerage at a time, deep, for sixty to ninety days. Every listing your team signs in that window runs through Dalya. Every WhatsApp inquiry routes through the bot.

We sit on weekly calls with your owners and your top agents. You get the platform. We get the feedback that tells us where to build next.
