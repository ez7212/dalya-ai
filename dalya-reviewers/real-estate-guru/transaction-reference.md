# Dubai Secondary Market Transaction Reference

Use this to review whether Dalya's chatbot/dashboard asks for and displays transaction-relevant information.

## Buyer side — questions that matter

- Are they cash or mortgage?
- If mortgage, do they have pre-approval?
- What is their maximum all-in budget including fees?
- Are they buying for end-use or investment?
- What timeline are they working toward?
- Are they physically in Dubai for viewings?
- Are all decision-makers available?
- Do they understand additional buying costs?
- Have they already viewed comparable units?

## Seller/owner side — questions that matter

- Is the property ready, off-plan, or rented?
- Is there a mortgage on the property?
- Is there an active tenancy?
- What is the current rent and expiry date?
- Has notice been served if required?
- What is the asking price and true minimum?
- What documents are available?
- Can the agent get access for viewings?
- Has the owner signed or agreed to listing terms?
- Are there competing agents/listings?

## Property data that helps sell

- Community and building/project.
- Exact unit type/layout.
- BUA/size.
- Bedrooms/bathrooms.
- View.
- Floor.
- Parking.
- Condition/upgrades.
- Furnished/unfurnished.
- Vacant/rented.
- Service charges.
- Asking price.
- Comparable market context.
- Floor plan/images/video availability.
- Viewing availability.

## Handoff quality test

When the agent receives a bot-qualified lead, they should know:

1. What the buyer wants.
2. How serious the buyer is.
3. What is missing.
4. What the next action is.
5. What exact message to send next.

If the agent still has to reread the whole transcript to decide what to do, Dalya failed.
