# Pre-pivot brand artifacts (archived 2026-05-15)

These three documents were authored in March 2026 for the **consumer-direct brokerage strategy** — Dalya as a marketplace where sellers list directly at 0.15% commission and buyers browse off-plan resale listings.

That strategy was retired on 2026-05-15 in favor of B2B AI infrastructure for brokerages. See [`/brand/STRATEGIC-PIVOT-2026-05-15.md`](../../brand/STRATEGIC-PIVOT-2026-05-15.md) for the full reasoning.

## What's here

| File | Original purpose |
|---|---|
| `BRAND_GUIDE.md` | Brand Identity System v1.1 — consumer-direct brand guide. Brand purpose ("make the UAE property market legible"), vision, mission, values, voice. Superseded by [`/brand/BRAND.md`](../../brand/BRAND.md) and the Phase 1–2 detail docs in `/brand/`. |
| `dalya-positioning-and-marketing.md` | Positioning + marketing strategy. The "1.15% total commission instead of 2%" pitch, "live on every portal in 30 minutes" funnel, content-marketing playbook. Superseded by [`/brand/STRATEGIC-PIVOT-2026-05-15.md`](../../brand/STRATEGIC-PIVOT-2026-05-15.md). |
| `mahoroba-website-copy.md` | Full website copy for dalya.ae as a consumer-direct site — Sell page, Buy page, How-It-Works, About, Listings, FAQ. The dual-audience hero copy and the SPA-upload funnel. Retired with the consumer-direct strategy. The dalya.ae homepage when rebuilt will lead with B2B positioning. |

## What survives

Substantively nothing in here maps directly onto the current B2B product. A few specific assets that *might* still be reusable:

- The Mahoroba Realty regulatory anchor language (RERA-licensed, Trakheesi partner, DLD-registered) in `mahoroba-website-copy.md` is still accurate — it underwrites the bot operationally even in B2B context.
- The competitive-market context paragraphs in `dalya-positioning-and-marketing.md` (Dubai off-plan resale being 63% of 2024 transactions, secondary off-plan resale being underserved) are still true facts, even if the strategy built on them changed.
- The brand-values articulation in `BRAND_GUIDE.md` (transparency, precision, accessibility) was rewritten as Trustworthy / Calm / Sharp during Phase 1 — the underlying ideas survived in different language.

Otherwise: read these only as historical reference. Do not import their voice, claims, or positioning into new B2B surfaces.
