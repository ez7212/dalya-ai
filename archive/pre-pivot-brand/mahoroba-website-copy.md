## SECTION: THE GAP DALYA WAS BUILT TO CLOSE

**H2:** Off-plan resale is not like selling a finished property. Most brokerages treat it as if it were.

Dubai's off-plan resale market involves a contract, not a key. The seller is transferring a legal position in a Sale and Purchase Agreement — proof of payment, entitlement to the unit, and a set of rights and obligations toward the developer. A buyer needs to understand the payment milestones, the NOC requirements, the handover timeline, and the transfer process. These details live in the SPA.

Traditional brokerage rewrites the SPA from memory and hopes the result is accurate. Buyers get approximations. Sellers get listings that drift further from the truth over time.

Dalya starts with the document. Ends with the close.

---

## SECTION: PLATFORM CAPABILITIES

**H2:** What Dalya does at every stage.

---

**SPA Parsing**

On upload, Dalya reads the full Sale and Purchase Agreement and extracts:
- Unit number and development
- Floor plan, area (sq ft / sq m), and orientation
- Payment milestones — paid and outstanding
- Developer entity (DLD-registered)
- NOC eligibility status
- Handover date and construction stage

This data becomes the listing. Not a summary — the structured facts from the document.

---

**Trakheesi Permit in Under 30 Minutes**

Dalya obtains a valid DLD advertising permit for each listing before it goes live. The permit number appears on every portal listing. No listing is published without one.

From SPA upload to live listing on Property Finder and Bayut: under 30 minutes.

---

**The Property Advisor**

Every listing is covered by Dalya's property advisor — an AI trained on Dubai's off-plan resale market and the specific details of each SPA.

It responds to buyer inquiries in under 60 seconds, in the buyer's language (English, Arabic, Russian, Mandarin, Hindi), with answers drawn from the actual SPA data. It does not speculate. When a question requires human judgment, it says so and routes to the RERA-certified agent of record.

Available 24 hours a day, including UAE public holidays.

---

**The Seller Dashboard**

Every inquiry, every conversation, every offer — visible to the seller in real time. Not a summary. The actual exchange. The seller sees what Dalya sees.

Notification settings are fully configurable:
- Every new inquiry
- When a buyer asks about price
- Only on written offers
- Dashboard-only — no notifications

---

**Digital Form A**

Every seller engagement begins with a DLD-mandated Form A, prepared by Dalya from the SPA data and signed digitally. No printing, no scanning, no courier.

---

**Non-Exclusive by Design**

Dalya does not require exclusivity. Sellers with existing agent relationships can list with Dalya simultaneously. Whichever party finds the buyer, their commission terms apply.

---

## SECTION: WHAT DALYA DOES NOT DO

**H2:** Where the licensed agent takes over.

Dalya handles the operational layer: parsing, listing, permit issuance, inquiry response, and offer routing. The RERA-certified agent of record handles:

- Valuation guidance and pricing strategy
- Negotiation on complex offers
- NOC coordination with developers
- DLD transfer paperwork and registration
- Any situation that requires professional judgment or legal accountability

Dalya does not give valuation opinions, mortgage advice, or legal counsel. These belong to qualified professionals.

## FOR SELLERS

**Q: Do I need to be in Dubai to list?**
A: No. SPA upload, Form A signing, and dashboard access are all remote. Most Dalya sellers are non-resident investors.

**Q: What format does my SPA need to be in?**
A: PDF is preferred. Dalya can parse most standard Dubai developer SPA formats. If your document is scanned or partially redacted, we flag issues before the listing goes live — not after.

**Q: How long does it actually take?**
A: Under 30 minutes from SPA upload to a live listing with a Trakheesi permit on Property Finder and Bayut, assuming the document is complete and legible.

**Q: Do I pay commission?**
A: No. In Dubai, brokerage commission is paid by the buyer. Your only cost is portal fees, passed through at cost with no markup.

**Q: What are the portal fees?**
A: Property Finder and Bayut charge listing fees based on duration and placement. Dalya passes these through at cost. You receive the exact figure before your listing is published.

**Q: I already have an agent. Can I still use Dalya?**
A: Yes. Dalya does not require exclusivity. If Dalya finds the buyer, Dalya's commission applies. If your other agent finds the buyer, their arrangement applies. No conflict.

**Q: Can I see who has inquired and what they asked?**
A: Yes. The seller dashboard shows every inquiry and every conversation in real time. Nothing is filtered.

**Q: Can I control when I get contacted?**
A: Yes. Notification thresholds range from "every new inquiry" to "written offers only." You can change your setting at any time.

**Q: Can I remove my listing?**
A: Yes, at any time from the dashboard. No fixed-term contracts.

**Q: What if my unit has an unresolved NOC issue?**
A: Dalya flags NOC status from the SPA. A listing with unresolved NOC issues will not go live without explicit disclosure. We discuss this with you first.

---

## FOR BUYERS

**Q: How is the property information different from what I see on other portals?**
A: Dalya listings are built from the seller's SPA — not written by an agent from memory. Payment plan details, unit specifications, and handover status reflect the legal document.

**Q: What is the total commission I pay?**

**Q: I have a buyer's agent. Does that affect anything?**
A: No. Your buyer's agent can co-broke with Dalya. Their 1% is unaffected. Your total is 1.15%.

**Q: What languages does Dalya respond in?**
A: English, Arabic (Modern Standard and Gulf dialect), Russian, Mandarin (Simplified), and Hindi. Language is detected automatically.

**Q: What if Dalya can't answer my question?**
A: Dalya tells you when something requires human judgment and routes to the RERA-certified agent of record. It does not speculate.

**Q: How do I make an offer?**
A: Through Dalya, at any time. A formal offer notifies the seller immediately regardless of their notification settings.

**Q: Can I view the property?**

**Q: Is Dalya a licensed brokerage?**
