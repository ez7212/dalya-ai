# Russian-language Demand & Supply Research — Dubai Off-Plan Resale

Scope: Russian-speaking sellers of Dubai off-plan property (primary target: Russian non-residents who bought 2022–2024 and need to exit before handover). Date: 2026-04-24. Language code: `ru`.

---

## 1. Summary — Russian-specific findings

Russian is the most asymmetric language opportunity among Dalya's target demographics: demand is demonstrably high and structurally rising, while supply of genuinely *seller-side* Russian content from licensed Dubai brokerages is thin and in several cases non-existent. Four unique dynamics shape the Russian seller-side picture:

1. **2022–2024 cohort is now exiting at scale.** Russian investor share of Dubai deals fell from 2.18% in 2022 (~1,068 deals) to 1.49% in the first five months of 2025 (758 deals), with Forbes.ru reporting average realized losses of 5–15% for those attempting quick resale. This is precisely Dalya's customer — someone who entered near peak, needs to exit before handover, and is cash-flow sensitive. [Forbes.ru — "Слезинка инвестора"](https://www.forbes.ru/biznes/540946-slezinka-investora-rossiane-teraut-den-gi-na-rastusem-rynke-nedvizimosti-dubaa)
2. **Tax / CRS / 3-НДФЛ questions are the hinge.** CRS automatic information exchange between the UAE and Russia has been live since 2019; non-residents face 30% НДФЛ in Russia; the double-tax treaty only partially offsets this. No Dubai brokerage is currently publishing in Russian at the intersection of "Dubai off-plan exit" + "how you are actually taxed back home" — that is the single largest content gap. [Irinauae.law — CRS обмен РФ–ОАЭ](https://irinauae.law/blog/crs-mezhdu-rf-i-oae-avtomaticheskij-obmen-i-chto-uvidit-fns) · [RBK Companies — CRS обмен](https://companies.rbc.ru/news/H9svcIMly2/avtomaticheskij-obmen-dannyimi-mezhdu-rossiej-i-oae-chto-nuzhno-znat/)
3. **Remote-selling is a distinctive Russian pain point.** Many Russian sellers cannot freely fly to Dubai (visa/sanctions/banking friction); the seller needs POA + UAE bank account in their own name (required since mid-2025 for all foreign sellers to receive proceeds). There is very limited Russian-language content explaining the POA + eNotary + bank-account-in-owner's-name stack end-to-end. [goldenbee.estate](https://goldenbee.estate/ru/blog/kak-prodavat-nedvizhimost-v-dubae) · [POA.ae](https://www.poa.ae/)
4. **Banking friction and "Russian exit"-flavoured content.** Most UAE banks apply enhanced due diligence on Russian nationals, and several refuse account opening — a blocker for getting paid on a sale. This is adjacent to tax but deserves its own content pillar; only Russian diaspora legal blogs (MIGRON, RBK, VC.ru) cover it, not the brokerages. [VC.ru — открыть счет ОАЭ](https://vc.ru/invest/2303504-kak-rossiyanam-otkryt-bankovskij-schet-v-oae)

Saturation at a glance (see §5 for detail): Generic "как купить в Дубае" is **saturated**. "Как продать off-plan / переуступка" is **partially served** (mostly by legal consultancies, not brokerages). Tax + CRS + 3-НДФЛ + remote-sale-from-Russia clusters are **empty or near-empty** from any licensed Dubai seller-side voice — this is where Dalya can own the category.

## 3. Topic clusters (Russian)

1. **Exit mechanics (переуступка / ассайнмент).** Min-% rules per developer, timing, Form F, NOC, DLD registration. [cluster weight: large]
2. **Tax — UAE side (4% DLD, 2%/2% split, VAT on commission, no CGT).** Moderate — reasonably covered by existing Russian pieces.
3. **Tax — Russian side (3-НДФЛ, 30% non-resident rate, 183-day rule, DTT with UAE, CRS exchange).** **Empty from brokerage voice.**
4. **Remote selling (POA, eNotary, no-travel sale flow).** Partially covered, never as an integrated end-to-end playbook.
5. **Banking / getting paid (UAE account in owner's name, Russian bank transfer, KYC, source-of-funds).** **Empty from brokerage voice.**
6. **Commission economics (2% + 5% VAT standard; hidden fees; who pays).** Well served by big portals in general but not reframed as "how to save commission when you're already underwater."
7. **Market psychology / exit timing (Dubai prices cooled; 2026 geopolitical shock; how to decide hold vs sell).** Some Russian coverage (Forbes, RBK, Kommersant) but nothing brokerage-authored.
8. **Developer-specific exit rules (Emaar 40%, Damac, Sobha, Nakheel, Azizi, Dubai Properties).** Fragmented. Each developer's NOC cost + min-paid % is searched but rarely published in Russian.
9. **"I am stuck" scenarios (can't make installments; developer delay; rescission under RERA).** Covered by a few legal blogs (vc.ru); no brokerage voice.
10. **Pricing & valuation (how to price your unit, district comparables).** Heavy buyer-side listings content; very thin seller-side valuation guidance in Russian.
11. **Documents & process (SPA/MOU/Form F/NOC/Title Deed/Oqood/BRN).** Fragmented; sellers must piece it together across 5+ articles.
12. **Sanctions / CIS banking friction.** Only covered by diaspora legal blogs, not Dubai brokerages.

## 6. Sanctions / tax / CRS observations shaping Russian seller content strategy

- **CRS is the elephant.** Russian–UAE automatic exchange has been live since 2019; a Russian tax resident selling Dubai off-plan through a UAE bank account is effectively reported to FNS. This is the single most-searched-yet-least-answered question on the Russian seller-side. Content must address it head-on; ducking it destroys trust. [irinauae.law](https://irinauae.law/blog/crs-mezhdu-rf-i-oae-avtomaticheskij-obmen-i-chto-uvidit-fns)
- **183-day / non-resident status is bidirectional trap.** Sellers who moved to Dubai think they are "not taxed by Russia" and are often wrong; sellers who stayed in Russia face 30% НДФЛ on worldwide income unless they structure correctly and use the DTT. Dalya content should explicitly break down both cases. [mnp.ru](https://www.mnp.ru/kejsy/inostrannoe-nalogooblozhenie-kejsy/regulirovanie-nalogov-dlja-rossijan-v-dubae-i-rossii/)
- **Mid-2025 rule: seller must receive proceeds in a UAE bank account in their own name.** This is a hard operational block for many Russian non-residents (enhanced KYC; account opening can take weeks and is often declined). Dalya content and ops must pre-qualify sellers on banking. [goldenbee.estate](https://goldenbee.estate/ru/blog/kak-prodavat-nedvizhimost-v-dubae)
- **9% UAE corporate tax can bite serial flippers.** If a Russian investor purchased multiple units with intent to resell, UAE FTA may treat this as business activity → 9% corporate tax. Almost no Russian-language brokerage content explains the threshold. [uae-consulting.com](https://www.uae-consulting.com/publikaczii/stati/nalogovaya-sistema-oae.html)
- **Sanctions-driven account-opening refusals** make Dalya's operational support (not just content) a differentiator. Positioning: "Мы не только продадим, мы проведем вас через открытие счёта и налоговую отчётность."
- **Trust bar localization.** Russian audiences respond strongly to explicit regulatory citations (RERA номер, DLD регистрация, Trakheesi permit). Dalya's trust bar elements translate well and should be kept literal, not loosened.
- **Content tone.** Russian real-estate audiences are skeptical of "cinematic" brokerage copy; they trust numbered lists, AED-exact figures, and named regulation (Law No. 8 of 2007 escrow, Form F, Oqood). The Dalya design-token guidance ("data is the hero") is a very good fit for Russian copy.
